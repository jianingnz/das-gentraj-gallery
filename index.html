Qualitative results + bench

Track-conditioned video generation, across five datasets

Diffusion-as-Shader (CogVideoX-5b-I2V base + rainbow 3D-track conditioning) fed our _GenTraj pipeline's tracks as the motion signal and a short caption as the content signal. The bench tabs compare DaS against Wan 2.2-TI2V-5B on 730 clips across five datasets; the noobj-bench tabs rerun the same comparison plus HOT3D-Aria with a "no new objects" prompt suffix; the qualitative tabs are the 451 track-conditioned triptychs.
