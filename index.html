Benchmarks

DaS P2 vs Wan2.2-TI2V-5B — DAVIS (n=3)

Three DAVIS clips (blackswan, snowboard, soapbox). Both models generate at 720×480, 49 frames, 15 fps; same image conditioning (source frame 0) and the same text prompt. DaS P2 additionally consumes rainbow 3D-track conditioning derived from world-frame _GenTraj tracks projected per-frame through the c2w pose; Wan uses text + first-frame image only.
