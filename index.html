Track-conditioned video generation, across five datasets

Diffusion-as-Shader (CogVideoX-5b-I2V base + rainbow 3D-track conditioning) fed our _GenTraj pipeline's tracks as the motion signal and a short caption as the content signal. Each card shows the source RGB, the tracks we rendered, and the generated clip, side-by-side.
